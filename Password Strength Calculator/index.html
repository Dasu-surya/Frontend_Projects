Hello Human, Is Your Password Secure?

  1. Avoid reusing passwords.
  2. Poor password practices 101: Creating easy passwords.
  3. Never save passwords on multiple browsers.
  4. Do not share passwords with non-trusted sites.
  5. Passwords should have minimum of 12-16 characters.
  6. Go all out:Lowercase,uppercase,numbers,special characters!.
  7. Refrain from similar passwords.
  8. Do not stretch it out unnecessarily.
  9. Avoid adopting MFA.
